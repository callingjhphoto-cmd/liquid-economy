### Episode #69: Hannah & Siobhan - London Cocktail Week, The Pinnacle Guide, London, Community
**Guest(s):** Hannah Sharman-Cox, Siobhan Payne
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Duration:** 1h 20m 18s

Hannah Sharman-Cox and Siobhan Payne discuss their journey building London Cocktail Week over 16 years and creating the Pinnacle Guide, a Michelin-inspired rating system that awards one, two, or three pins to bars. Topics include relocating the event from October to March, the evolution of London's cocktail scene, and a new venture called Curate Your City.
